Our Purpose

Heavenly Travels was started having these main beliefs in mind.

Promote Wellness

We believe in promoting international reproductive healthcare, sustainable agriculture, and other fundamental human rights throughout our travels.

Creative Solutions

One of the best aspects of traveling to developing regions is how resourcefulness leads to innovative and creative solutions to promote sustainability and prosperity.

Responsible Travel

We believe in respecting the dignity of the people we meet and in having honest dialogues about the unique circumstances our friends face.
